Project Overview

Anne's Bakery is a design for online bakery shopping website. This project was done during the completing of Google UX Design Certificate.

Anne's Bakery website is a responsive website designed to help users to purchase items in the shop much easier, whether they choose to dine-in or take away their orders. With the goal of connecting users with the love for coffees and pastries to the bakery itself, Anne's Bakery ordering website ensures that users can easily create their orders with just a few taps.
